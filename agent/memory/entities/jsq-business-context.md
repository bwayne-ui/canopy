# JSQ Business Context

## About Juniper Square

Juniper Square (JSQ) is an investment management software and services company serving private markets GPs and LPs.

## Revenue Lines

| Line | Description | Current Scale |
|---|---|---|
| **GPX (Software)** | SaaS platform for GPs: LP portal, reporting, capital call/distribution processing, document management, data rooms | $70M+ ARR, 2,000+ clients |
| **Fund Administration** | Outsourced fund accounting, NAV calculation, investor reporting, tax (K-1), waterfall, compliance | 480+ GP clients, 3,000 entities |
| **Loan Administration** | Loan-level accounting and reporting for private credit funds | Growing line |

## Fund Administration Business Model

- **Pricing**: BPS (basis points) on AUA (assets under administration). Typically 8–25 bps depending on fund size, complexity, and service level. Annual minimum fee floors per entity.
- **Delivery model**: TL hierarchy (TL0 → TL1 → TL2 → TL3 → TL4+ ICs). Each TL2 director manages a portfolio of clients with TL3 managers and ICs.
- **Systems clients use**: Investran, Allvue/AltaReturn, eFront, Geneva/SS&C, Yardi, JSQ platform itself.

## Typical GP Services Scope
- Monthly or quarterly NAV calculation per entity
- Capital call processing (notices, LP allocations, wire instructions)
- Distribution processing (waterfall calculations, tax withholding)
- Quarterly LP statements
- Annual financial statements and audit support
- Annual K-1 preparation (US tax)
- FATCA/CRS compliance and reporting
- Management fee and carried interest calculations
- AML/KYC on new LP subscriptions
- Data room management

## Competitive Context
JSQ competes with: SS&C GlobeOp, Citco, Alter Domus, IQ-EQ, Apex Group, Intertrust/CSC, Sievert Larson Lynge (smaller shops). Differentiator is deep software + services integration — one platform for GP workflow + FA delivery.
